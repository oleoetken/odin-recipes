Pizza

Description

Are you getting hungry seeing this one too?

Don't worry we got you covered!

Follow the steps below to make an original italian pizza margharita yourself!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Fresh Morzarella Cheese
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough
  2. Put tomato sauce on
  3. Use a bit of salt
  4. Add the morzarella cheese
  5. Put in the oven for 20 minutes at 180 degrees
