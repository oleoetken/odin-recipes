Pasta

Description

Tasty pasta in a few minutes!

Learn how to do it in the following steps!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Salt
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot and add salt
  2. When water is boiling add the noodles and let them cook for 10-15 minutes
  3. Serve them on a plate and add the pasta
