Pancakes

Description

This recipe will show you how to make delicious pancakes!

So let's start!

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • A bit sunflower oil

Steps

  1. Put all in a bowl with a bit salt and whisk to a smooth batter
  2. Start cooking
  3. Cook each side for 1 minute, then flip them again until each side is golden
